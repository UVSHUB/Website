About Nutzy Craft

We are Sri Lanka’s first dedicated freelance ecosystem, bridging the gap between exceptional local talent and global businesses.

Who We Are

Nutzy Craft is more than just a job board. We are a managed ecosystem designed to eliminate the uncertainties of freelancing. Born from a desire to showcase Sri Lanka's world-class IT talent, we provide a structured, secure, and quality-driven platform for businesses to build, hire, and scale.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To empower Sri Lankan IT professionals with global opportunities while providing businesses with reliable, high-quality technical solutions.

Vision: To become the leading digital workforce platform in South Asia, known for trust, innovation, and community.

Our Values

Trust

We believe transparency is the foundation of every successful project. Our secure payment and verification systems ensure peace of mind.

Quality

We don't just match; we verify. Our "Managed Projects" feature ensures that every deliverable meets international standards.

Innovation

We are constantly evolving. From AI matching to our upcoming mobile apps, we use technology to make work easier.

Fairness

We champion fair rates for freelancers and value-driven pricing for clients. Everyone wins in the Nutzy Craft ecosystem.

The Future

We are just getting started. As Sri Lanka's first dedicated freelance app, we are rolling out mobile applications, Sinhala/Tamil language support, and an AI-driven project management assistant to revolutionize how you work.

ABOUT US

Separated by distance but connected by vision, Nutzy Craft stands at the frontier of digital innovation.

We do not just observe the changing tides of technology; we are the current. In this large ocean of freelance challenges, we build the bridges of code and design that ensure no idea goes unrealized.

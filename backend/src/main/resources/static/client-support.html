Frequently Asked Questions

How do I hire a freelancer?
Browse freelancers or post a job. Once you receive proposals, you can click "Hire Now" to start the contract and fund the milestones securely.
Is my payment secure?
Yes, all payments are processed through our secure gateway (Stripe/PayPal) and held in escrow until you approve the work submitted by the freelancer.
How can I dispute a job?
If you are unsatisfied with the work, you can raise a dispute from the "My Jobs" page. Our support team will review the case and mediate a resolution.
Can I edit my job post?
Yes, you can edit your job post from the "My Jobs" dashboard as long as you haven't hired anyone yet.

Need Urgent Help?

Our support team is available 24/7 to assist you.

How It Works

Simple, secure, and transparent processing for everyone.

For Clients

Hire with confidence in 5 simple steps.

01

Submit Project Requirements

Post a job with your specific needs, timeline, and budget. Our guided forms make this easy.

02

Review & Plan

NutzyCraft reviews your request. For managed projects, we create a detailed roadmap and milestone plan.

03

Assign Freelancers

Our AI matches you with the perfect expert, or we assign a dedicated team for enterprise needs.

04

Project Execution

Monitor progress via your dashboard. Communicate directly and track milestones in real-time.

05

Quality Check & Delivery

We verify the output against your requirements. Funds are released only when you are satisfied.

For Freelancers

Build your career on your terms.

1. Apply & Verify

Create your profile and pass our skill verification to earn the "Verified" badge.

2. Get Matched

Receive job invitations that match your skills, or browse open projects.

3. Deliver Quality

Work with structured guidance and clear requirements.

4. Get Paid

Enjoy secure, on-time payments directly to your bank account.
